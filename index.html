Welcome to U TURN

U TURN is a platform dedicated to connecting local and global trash vendors to facilitate the buying, selling, and recycling of waste materials. Join us in our mission to promote sustainable development and reduce waste!

Project Details

Our platform allows users to:

  • Buy and sell recyclable materials.
  • Connect with local vendors and recycling centers.
  • Track demand for various types of waste.
  • Promote sustainable practices in waste management.
