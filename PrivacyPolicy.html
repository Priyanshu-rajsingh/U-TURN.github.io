Privacy Policy

At U Turn, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you use our platform. This includes, but is not limited to, the following:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number.
  • Details about the waste you list or search for.
  • Transaction details when you engage with buyers or sellers.
  • Data about your usage of our platform (e.g., IP address, browsing behavior).

2. How We Use Your Information

The personal information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Facilitate transactions between waste sellers and buyers.
  • Communicate with you about your account and transactions.
  • Send promotional and informational emails (with your consent).
  • Ensure the security of our platform and protect against fraud.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to ensure the safety of your personal information. These include encryption technologies and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information with trusted partners to:

  • Provide services on our behalf (e.g., payment processing, data analysis).
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of U Turn, our users, and others.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the methods outlined in the "Contact Us" section of our website.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your user experience on our website. Cookies help us understand how you use our platform and allow us to provide features like saving preferences and tracking session data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or change this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
